What is Hiber-Zone?



Hiber-Zone helps you to process and understand your emotions.
It aims to provide a user friendly and assist the user according to their pace.

We follow the motto: RELAX, RECOVER, RESTART. Where the user can choose to improve their well being and state of mind based on their mood and feelings.
Our focus is to create a safe space for people to take a break, consult professionals to understand their state of mind, and get back to productivity.
